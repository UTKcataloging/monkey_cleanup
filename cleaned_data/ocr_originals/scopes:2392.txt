Friday AM

Dear Sue

I left Dayton before Court adjourned Wednesday - and now desire to thank you for the consideration shown me Express my thanks to HE Gen McKenzie, Judge McK and to Mr Haggard for making it possible for me to enjoy the day.

You gentlemen are ably handling the "Wise Men of the East" 

Bully for Stewart and the Bryans

Very sincerely 

W.A. [illegible]
