July 2nd 1925.The Hon. Wm Jennings Bryan. Dear Mr. Bryan,I am very grateful for your [illegible] generous interest. Kindly receive the enclosed check for half the price of the two lots less ten per cent.   Am sure you are to come triumphantly through on the 10th inst.Mrs. Wedderspoon sends love to Mrs. Bryan.I am Yrs Faithfully,W.R. Wedderspoon
